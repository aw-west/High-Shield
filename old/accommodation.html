Accommodation

Old High Shield Cottage is a spacious single storey building that provides self-catered accommodation for two people. The cottage offers visitors exceptional views south over the excavations of Vindolanda, then up to Thorngrafton Common and onwards over the rolling hills of the counties of Northumberland, Durham and Cumbria. The accommodation includes a double bed, a fully fitted kitchen, separate shower and bath, traditional fireplace, smart TV and wireless internet,
