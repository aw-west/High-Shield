Activities - Things to do near Old High Shield

Hexham

The bustling market town of Hexham is situated in the Tyne valley 12 miles from Old High Shield. Hexham Abbey dates from 674AD and is a must for any visitor.

The town's market is held every Tuesday. It has a wide variety of pubs and restaurants, the Queen’s Hall Arts Centre, a museum, library, cinema and theatre.

It has a good variety of shops including Waitrose, Tesco’s and Marks & Spencer and food can be ordered online for delivery to Old High Shield.

For more information visit: Hexham Tourist Website.
