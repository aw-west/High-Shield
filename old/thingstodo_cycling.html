Activities - Things to do near Old High Shield

Cycling

There are numerous local routes for cyclists and the Stanegate route is popular.

The Hadrian's Cycleway (National Route 72) runs from Ravenglass to South Shields via the Cumbrian Coast, Carlisle and Hexham.

The Pennine Cycleway (National Route 68) runs from Derby to Berwick-upon-Tweed, however enters Northumberland from Cumbria. The route finishes at Berwick-on-Tweed after running through Haltwhistle, Bellingham and Wooler. Bicycles can be rented from us.
